# Method 5 Concept Stakeholder Alignment

## Concept 1: Coordinated Damage Feedback

**Purpose**: Validate that visual differentiation between normal and combo damage creates satisfying, legible feedback for coordinated kaiju attacks.

**Validation Target**: 
- Kaiju players understand that synchronized attacks = bigger impact (visually)
- The effect feels "rewarding" without requiring tutorial explanation
- Non-players can immediately see that *something special* happened on the combo

**Stakeholder Perspectives to Test**:
- Kaiju player: "Does this make me want to coordinate again?"
- First-time player: "Can I understand what just happened without being told?"
- Spectators (room): "Does the combo pop feel like a payoff moment?"

---

## Concept 2: Score Reveal & Leaderboard

**Purpose**: Validate that displaying the HUMANS SAVED count + leaderboard creates a compelling social payoff and drives replayability motivation.

**Validation Target**:
- All kaiju feel they contributed to a shared outcome (not zero-sum competition)
- The leaderboard creates a natural "one more round?" moment
- The ceremony feels like an ending (not abrupt) and a beginning (not blocking next match)

**Stakeholder Perspectives to Test**:
- Kaiju players: "Do I want to beat my previous best? Do I want to help the team beat the record?"
- Commander: "Does seeing my initials up there feel rewarding?"
- Spectators: "Does this make me want to play next?"
