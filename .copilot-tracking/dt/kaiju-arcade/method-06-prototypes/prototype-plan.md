# Method 6: Prototype Planning

## Core Assumption

**"Players naturally discover emergent coordinated tactics based on attack patterns and positioning. Sniper (ranged, single-target) vs. Dozer (close-range, area damage) will produce different group strategies and reward positioning-based synchronization."**

## What We're Testing

Not: *Does damage pop feel rewarding?*

**Yes**: *What positioning and timing strategies emerge when players have different attack patterns they can coordinate?*

---

## Prototype Approach: Narrated Combat Scenario

**Format**: Markdown role-play + simultaneous action calls

**Participants**: 
- Coach (me): Narrator + game state authority
- Players (you + simulated kaiju): Call out simultaneous actions each round

**Scenario Flow**:
1. Roster introduced (mix of Snipers and Dozers)
2. Combat state established (target health, round timer, positions)
3. Round 1: Players call simultaneous actions (attack target, move position)
4. Outcome resolved (damage calculated, combos detected by pattern overlap)
5. Repeat for 3-4 rounds until interesting patterns emerge

**Build Time**: ~20 minutes setup + ~8 mins per round played

---

## Kaiju Roster (Simplified)

Testing two attack pattern types:

### Sniper
- **Range**: Long (can attack from distance)
- **Damage Pattern**: Single-target, concentrated
- **Normal Attack**: 12 damage to one target
- **Vibe**: Precision, can focus fire or spread damage

### Dozer
- **Range**: Short (must be close)
- **Damage Pattern**: Area cone, hits multiple targets
- **Normal Attack**: 8 damage to all enemies in cone
- **Vibe**: Brawler, rewards clustering and protecting teammates in the damage zone

---

## Test Scenario

**Setup**:
- 4 players (mix: 2 Snipers, 2 Dozers, OR 3 Snipers + 1 Dozer — we'll try variations)
- Commander defending city with limited defensive assets
- 90-second match, tracked in 15-second "phases"
- Objective: Kaiju coordinate to reach the city; Commander tries to delay

**Observation Points**:
1. Do Snipers focus-fire one target or spread damage?
2. Do Dozers move together or scatter?
3. What happens when two attacks hit the same target simultaneously (combo)?
4. Do players naturally protect Dozers (who have to get close)?
