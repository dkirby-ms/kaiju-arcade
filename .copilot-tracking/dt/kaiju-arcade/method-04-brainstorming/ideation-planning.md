# Method 04 — Ideation Planning

> Session setup and constraints. 2026-06-23.

## Divergent Target

**20 rough ideas** before convergence. One-liners; no detail; parking lot for later.

## HMW Driving Questions (All 12)

From Method 3 synthesis themes:

**Theme 1 — Two Modes of Fun**
- HMW-01: Design Commander's triage loop for meaningful decisions
- HMW-02: Design kaiju roster so each archetype's combo role is obvious
- HMW-03: Ensure both roles feel complete in 90 seconds

**Theme 2 — The Room Is the Third Mechanic**
- HMW-04: Verbal coordination ("attack NOW!") triggers combos naturally
- HMW-05: Make Commander screen watchable as spectator sport
- HMW-06: Reduce setup friction to under 60 seconds

**Theme 3 — Managed Retreat as Emotional Arc**
- HMW-07: Present evacuation score as achievement, not consolation
- HMW-08: Design high score entry as social ceremony
- HMW-09: Give kaiju a sense of personal contribution in collective win

**Theme 4 — Legibility Over Depth**
- HMW-10: Communicate rocket launch readiness and risk at a glance
- HMW-11: Kaiju ability system learnable in first 15 seconds
- HMW-12: First round works as a tutorial

## Constraints as Creative Drivers

- **90-second round duration**: hard time cap; forces ruthless prioritization
- **No tutorial**: every mechanic must be legible at first glance; shape design for discoverability
- **The room is the mechanic**: verbal coordination in shared space; spectator reactions matter; design for noise
- **Commander plays full match**: no respawns; sustained cognitive load; pressure stays high
- **Azure Table Storage leaderboard**: persistence, initials entry (classic arcade), all-time best runs visible

## Perspectives to Explore

1. **Commander perspective**: solo optimization, bittersweet scoring, triage under pressure
2. **Kaiju collective perspective**: emergent coordination, archetype variety, combo discovery
3. **Spectator perspective**: eliminated players, room observers — what keeps them engaged?
4. **First-time player perspective**: no manual; what must be immediately obvious?

## AI Collaboration Pattern

**Silent Observer** → **Constraint Catalyst** → **Divergent Facilitator**. User generates ideas; coach surfaces patterns and reframes constraints as creative drivers.

## Session Structure

1. Warm-up: one idea per HMW (12 ideas) to explore each dimension
2. Freeform divergence: 8–10 more ideas using constraint rotation and perspective shifting
3. Convergence: cluster by philosophy and HMW alignment
