## Convergence: Philosophy-Based Clustering

### Cluster A: Scarcity & Irreversibility (Forces Meaningful Decisions)
- Idea #1: Assets permanent
- Idea #3: Fixed asset pool per round
- Idea #11: Rocket prep window creates defend-or-launch tradeoff

**Philosophy**: Every choice has a cost. No infinite resources. Triage is genuine.

---

### Cluster B: Legibility Through Visual Design (Self-Teaching Mechanics)
- Idea #2: Archetype silhouette + color coding
- Idea #6: 80s vectorized dashboard aesthetic
- Idea #12: Ability learnable immediately by using it
- Idea #13: First round is the tutorial

**Philosophy**: Shape teaches function. No menus, no manuals. Discovery-first.

---

### Cluster C: Coordinated Asymmetry (Room Energy)
- Idea #4: Generous simultaneous-attack combo window
- Idea #5: Normal + limited specials; specials combo
- Idea #7: Colyseus lobby flow (fast onboarding)

**Philosophy**: Verbal coordination is the mechanic. The room is the third player.

---

### Cluster D: Score as Social Achievement (Consequence-Free Replayability)
- Idea #8: "HUMANS SAVED" reveal + initials entry
- Idea #9: Leaderboard ceremony (live, highlighted entry)
- Idea #10: Kaiju personal stats (damage %, combos)

**Philosophy**: Every player sees their contribution. Defeat = personal achievement. Leaderboard is the reason to play again.
